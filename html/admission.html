Secondary School Admission


Our admission process for the secondary section is straight-forward and only 3 entry points are available (Form 1,2 and 3),

Form 1

Form 1 contestants have to go through the interview process which occurs a few weeks after the Government Commonn Entrance (GCE)
They would have to bring the following for the interview which will include a test:

  1. Common Entrance Slip
  2. Common Entrance Results Page
  3. Photocopy of Birth Certificate
  4. Class 6 report card
  5. Writing Materials
  6. Interview Fee
  7. Writing Materials

Form 2 and Form 3

Form 2 and 3 contestants have to simply bring their report cards of the previous class and they will be accessed depending on their
performance in their previous class.


High School Admission

Our admission process for the High School section is straight-forward and only a single entry point is available (Lower Sixth),

Lower Sixth

Lower-Sixth contestants have to go through the interview process which occurs a one or two months after the GCE Ordinary Level
They would have to bring the following for the interview:

  1. Ordinary Level Slip
  2. Photocopy of Birth Certificate
  3. Interview Fee
