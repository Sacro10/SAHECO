MOTTO: INTERGRITY-SERVICE-EXCELLENCE
Mr. Numen Yousi Georges
Former Vice-Principal/Dean
numenyousige@gmail.com

Born in Bamenda Mezam Division. Numen Yousi Georges attended St. Joseph's Catholic Boys school Big Mankon,
Sacred Heart College, Mankon, then the university of Yaounde where he graduated with a BSc. After teaching
for many years in Sacred Heart College, mankon, he entered ENS second cycle under the University of Bamenda
where he did Science of Education and obtained a DIPET II. A few years later, he enrolled at the university
of Dschang where he obtained an MSc. He later travelled to the U.S. where he attended Bali State University
Indiana, U.S.A. and obtained a Certificate in Social Enterpreneurship.

He is a certified catechist, graduate of MaryVale Institute of Catechisis Birmingham Annex Bamendakwe.
He was trained by the British Council in areas of Child Safeguarding, Child Abuse, Special Need Education,
Child Protection, Fire Fighting and Safety and was awarded certificates in all these courses.
He is the assistant chief examiner of 0570 O Level Mathematics.
Numen Yousi Georges has been an infuential part in SAHECO's history with over a decade of experience gained
from his time in this establishment.

Mr. Georges's journey in education began at Sacred Heart College, an urban high school in the North west Region,
where he served diligently for decades. His extensive experience in both national and international curricula
has equipped him with a deep understanding of edcational best practices and innovative teaching methodologies.

Throughout his career, he has demonstrated exemplary leadership and a steadfast commitment to the holistic development
of his students. As a class master and house master for over 12 years, he led by example, fostering a culture of academic
excellence, sportmanship, and a personal growth among his students.
Under his leadership, previously average-performing classes were transformed into top performing ones, reflecting his
dedication to maximizing student learning outcomes. His expertise in student discipline and pastoral care further
contributed to the overall transformation of Sacred Heart College, Mankon into a beacon of excellence.

During his tenure as Vice Principal from 2007-2023, he played a pivotal role in elevating teh school's reputation nationally
and internationally. Under his guidance, the institution gained recognition as an inspiring, innovative and creative learning
environment, garnering accolates and praise from educational authorities and stakeholders alike.

As a prolific writer, He has authored several mathematics textbooks that are widely used across the nation. His passion for
agriculture and environmental sustainability has also left an indelible mark on his stidents, inspiring them to embrace farming
and conservation practices.
